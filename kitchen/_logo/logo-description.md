### Mood

This is the important part.

It should not feel like:

> "Look at this funny joke."

It should feel like:

> "These two finally found each other."

Exactly the feeling after reading your README:

> *"Zig Io makes developers' lives even more interesting. Matryoshka is an attempt to make them a little more boring."*

This image says the same thing without a single word.

---

One idea I particularly like is to have the **last can** barely visible at the very edge of the image, swinging so that only the orange Zig color is visible and the version text is hidden. People will notice the sequence ending at **0.16**, then realize there's another can whose version can't quite be read. It's a subtle little wink to the community rather than an explicit joke.
